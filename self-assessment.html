Professional Self-Assessment

Completing the Computer Science program at SNHU and developing my ePortfolio has been a transformative experience. Through rigorous coursework and hands-on projects, I have strengthened my technical foundation while cultivating skills in problem-solving, software design, and system development. The process of creating and enhancing the Android inventory management application exemplifies my ability to design maintainable, efficient, and scalable software solutions.

Collaboration has been a key aspect of my development as a professional. While working on a group project to build a card game, "21," in Android Studio, I coordinated feature implementation and task delegation among three team members. This experience honed my ability to communicate technical requirements clearly, balance responsibilities, and ensure that the project progressed smoothly toward completion.

My education has provided extensive exposure to data structures, algorithms, software engineering principles, and database management. Implementing the ShoppingListService using a priority queue and restructuring the inventory application’s database demonstrates my practical skills in algorithmic thinking, efficient data handling, and relational database design. These experiences reflect my capability to bridge theoretical knowledge with applied solutions in a real-world context.

Security has also been a critical area of focus. Participation in the National Cybersecurity League in 2022–2023 strengthened my awareness of secure coding practices, authentication strategies, and the importance of safeguarding data integrity. Combined with my software development experience, this knowledge enhances my preparedness to design systems that are both functional and secure.

My professional goal is to pursue a career as a project manager, leveraging my technical expertise to guide teams and oversee projects effectively. The artifacts presented in this ePortfolio demonstrate my proficiency across core areas of computer science and collectively illustrate my ability to design, implement, and manage technology solutions. This portfolio serves as a formal introduction to my skills, experience, and readiness to contribute meaningfully in the field of computer science.
